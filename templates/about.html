About Page

This is for more info on the VoicePlay webapp

  • Text-to-speech (TTS) is a type of technology that reads digital text aloud.
  • It's sometimes called “read aloud” technology.
  • With a click of a button, it can take words on a computer and convert them into audio.

What we plan

  • We aim to make a web interface where users can use a simple TTS service.
  • It will consist of many options, to select a specific voice type, an accent type, language, gender etc.
  • It will be a multi page web interface, linked with buttons, using Javascript, and designed with CSS.
